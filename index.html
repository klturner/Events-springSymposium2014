Brent Nussbaum – CEO, Nussbaum Transportation Services, Inc.

Brent has held many roles with Nussbaum, a sixty-nine year old family owned and operated company founded by his father in 1945. He began his career in equipment maintenance in the family’s LTL/regional truckload business from 1970-1974. Upon graduation from high school, he began driving a truck and then transitioned into the operations department.
